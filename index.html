Let’s Learn Photography!





One day photography session that includes a practical session and covers the following :

  • History and Transformation of Photography.
  • Basics and Fundamentals of Camera and Photography.
  • Light and The Photographer.
  • Human Eye and the Photographic Lenses.
  • Visualization of an Image composition and recomposition.
  • EXPOSURE TRIANGLE –Aperture , ISO , Shutter speed and its combination.
  • Depth , White Balance and Focusing.
  • Noise of field and lenses.
  • Making the difference and capturing the moment.
Course fee - Rs.999/-
